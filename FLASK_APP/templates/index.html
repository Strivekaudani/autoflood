AutoFlood

Automated Irrigation Solutions

AutoFlood is a web application for monitoring a Programmable Logic Controller(PLC) based automatic flood irrigation system. It makes use of soil moisture sensors to detect when to irrigate, and water level sensors to ensure that the water tank is always full. This optimizes the irrigation process which effectivly increases your yield.
